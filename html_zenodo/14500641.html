Description

ATLANTIC SPATIAL is a data set when we make available more than 500 raster layers with a fine-scale spatial information (resolution = 30 m) for the entire Atlantic Forest extent for the year 2020. Hydrological metrics comprise potential springs and its kernel and potential streams and respective distances.
